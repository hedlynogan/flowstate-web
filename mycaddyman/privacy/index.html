MyCaddyMan ("the app") is a golf assistant developed by Flow State Technologies ("we," "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles and why. We built MyCaddyMan to give you a number and get out of the way — and we took the same approach to your data.

In short: MyCaddyMan uses your location to calculate on-course distances, and that processing happens on your device. We don't sell your data, and we don't require an account to use the app.
