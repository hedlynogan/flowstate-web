Build: Trip Pause + Round Trip Date: July 13, 2026
Build Focus: Introduces Trip Pause, one-tap Round Trips with a duration prompt, and a cluster of trip-creation fixes.

This build adds TRIP PAUSE and smarter ROUND TRIPS, plus fixes to how trips home get their time and location.

TRIP PAUSE (new — please hammer this one)

  • During any trip, choose PAUSE 1 HOUR wherever you can extend (the check-in screen, the extend sheet, or a deadline notification)
  • Your deadline moves out an hour and the widget shows Paused
  • Let a pause RUN OUT without touching the app → you should get the normal check-in countdown and then the alert flow — never a silently ended trip
  • Premium: your emergency contact timing follows the pause too

ROUND TRIP — ONE TAP, OUT AND BACK

  • Tap the Round Trip tile → pick how long you’ll be (30 m / 1 h / 2 h / 4 h or dial your own) → Start
  • The button says “Getting your location…” until the phone has a FRESH fix — the trip must anchor to where you ARE, not where you last had signal
  • Try it right after driving somewhere: the return point should be your real starting spot
  • Trips are capped at 24 hours — for longer adventures, start a new trip

TRIP HOME — NOW ALWAYS ONE-WAY WITH A REAL ETA

  • Tap Trip Home (or pick Home inside the new-trip screen) → it’s a ONE-WAY trip and the arrival time comes from the actual travel time — watch the caption under the time pickers say where the suggestion came from
  • Set a time by hand, then wait a few seconds → your time must NOT get replaced by the suggestion
  • Arriving at home should COMPLETE the trip (no lingering countdown afterwards!)
  • Just finished a trip somewhere else? Start a Trip Home right after — the drive time should be from where you are now, not from where that last trip ended

NEW TRIP SCREEN — REORDERED

Destination first (with Home & Favorites quick picks), then Round Trip / One Way, then travel mode, and the arrival time appears LAST — only once there’s something to base it on.

STILL IN THIS BUILD (from last release)

Home/Lock Screen widgets (Trip Status + Quick Start), Premium subscription with 1-week free trial (sandbox — you will NOT be billed), Route Weather + AI briefing cards.

Feedback we want

  • Did a paused trip ever end silently or alert at the wrong time?
  • Did any trip anchor to the wrong spot or suggest a nonsense arrival time?
  • Did your hand-set time ever get overwritten?
  • Anything confusing in the new trip screen order?
