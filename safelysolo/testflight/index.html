Joining the Beta

The SafelySolo beta is currently invite-only and runs on iOS via TestFlight. If you aren't already part of the beta, you can join by following the official link provided in our beta invitation email — just make sure you have the TestFlight app installed on your iPhone first. Want an invite? Email hello@flowstateconsulting.com with "Beta Invite" in the subject line. An Android version is in development.

What to expect

Beta builds are where we test new features before they hit the general public. You may encounter bugs, and features might change rapidly. Your feedback helps us build a more reliable safety tool for everyone.
