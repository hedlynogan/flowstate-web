What to Test (2026-06-30)

Build: Premium Intro Date: June 30, 2026
Build Focus: Introduces SafelySolo Premium (subscription tier) and the Free vs Premium split.

⚠️ Known limitation: Subscribing is not active yet in this build. Tapping "Start Free Trial" will show "Subscription product is not available." Just explore the difference for now.

What to Test

This build introduces SafelySolo Premium. Please focus on understanding what's Free vs Premium and whether the split is clear and fair.

Free — Works without a subscription

  • Create and run trips (one-way, Round trip from here, Trip Home).
  • Live tracking, automatic arrival, and check-in reminders.
  • Adding trusted contacts.
  • If you miss a check-in: Prompted to text contacts from your own Messages.
  • Siri: Check in / extend / cancel an active trip.

Premium — Shows a lock / "Premium" badge

  • Automatic alerts: SafelySolo texts contacts automatically if you miss a check-in.
  • AI safety briefing + "Draft with AI" message.
  • Route weather.
  • Trusted Safety Contacts (consent / opt-in).
  • Hands-free trip start via Siri.

Please Check

  1. Are AI, Route weather, and Automatic alerts clearly marked Premium (locked rows)?
  2. Does the Premium upsell appear and clearly list the benefits when tapping a locked feature?
  3. Is it obvious what you get Free vs Premium?
  4. Does Siri prompt to go Premium when trying to start a trip via voice?
