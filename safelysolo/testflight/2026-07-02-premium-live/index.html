What to Test (2026-07-02)

Build: Premium Live & Signal Fix Date: July 2, 2026
Build Focus: Premium purchases are now active (sandbox) and trip tracking is improved for weak GPS signals.

1. Premium Subscription is Live (Sandbox)

Purchases now run in Apple's sandbox: no real money is ever charged.

  • Go to Settings → Premium (or tap a locked feature) → Start Free Trial.
  • Complete the purchase → features should unlock immediately.
  • Test Restore Purchases after a reinstall.

2. Improved Tracking in Weak GPS Signal

The app now keeps tracking with reduced accuracy instead of stopping in "dead zones."

  • Look for the orange "weak GPS signal" indicator in the map header.
  • Test a trip through a known poor-signal spot (canyons, tall buildings, dense trees).
  • Confirm the map trail keeps updating and the trip completes correctly.

3. Premium Trip Cards (New)

With Premium active, AI Safety Briefing and Route Weather cards appear automatically during trip review.

  • Confirm Route Weather loads real forecasts.
  • Test the Disable button on cards (they should stay off until re-enabled in Settings).

Feedback we want

  1. Did the trial/purchase flow feel smooth and trustworthy?
  2. Did tracking hold up in your known dead zones?
  3. Did weather load correctly?
