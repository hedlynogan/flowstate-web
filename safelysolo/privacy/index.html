SafelySolo ("the app") is a personal-safety app developed by Flow State Technologies ("we," "us"). It lets you set a check-in timer before doing something alone; if you don't check in, the app notifies contacts you've chosen and shares your location with them. Because that involves sensitive information, this policy is specific about what happens and when.

In short: the contacts you pick and your location are used to send a safety alert only when a check-in lapses or you trigger one, and that alert goes only to the contacts you chose. Text messages are delivered through a third-party messaging provider. We don't sell your data.
