MyCaddyMan

Your pocket caddy for smarter rounds.

Clear yardages to the front, center, and back of every green, simple shot tracking, and a scorecard that stays out of the way so you can keep your head in the round. Built for golfers who want the number, not the noise.

Quick tips

  • Add your home course before your first round so distances are ready the moment you reach the first tee.
  • Let location stay "While Using the App" — it's what powers live green distances, and it switches off when you close the app.
  • Tap a green to see front / center / back yardages; swipe to move down the hole.

SafelySolo

Check in, or get help, without overthinking it.

Set a check-in timer before a hike, a run, or a late walk to the car. Check in and nothing happens. Miss it, and SafelySolo texts your trusted contacts with a message and your location — so going it alone doesn't mean going it unwatched.

Quick tips

  • Add two or three trusted contacts, then send a test alert so everyone knows what a real one looks like.
  • Give location and SMS permission during setup — both are needed to send your position when a timer lapses.
  • Set the timer a little longer than you think you'll need; you can always check in early.
